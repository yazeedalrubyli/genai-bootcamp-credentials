How to verify a credential

Every credential is a W3C Verifiable Credential (Open Badges 3.0), signed with the issuer's Ed25519 key. When you open a recipient's credential page, your browser verifies the signature live against the issuer's public key published at did.json — so authenticity does not depend on any company staying in business.
